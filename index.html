Full-stack Designer

I've never been great with descibing myself, or what I do, since both change constantly. I can in turn summarize the past decade of my life, ending with a "where am I now" segment.

Graphic Design is my passion. Design of any kind is a small part of the vast list of things that interest me. I consider myself a jack of most trades. Pretty much anything in the creative field, with the exception being music (since I've never done anything musically, professionally). Most of what I do is self taught, so it took a lot of trial and error to get to where I am today. I dabble in illustration (traditional pen and paper, baby. Sorry... I dont normally say baby...that was awkward), digital art, photo manipulation, typography, video production, programming (android platform, besides the web), h4xing the mainframe, and other things I'm legally unauthorized to disclose.

Aside from the obvious, in my spare time I write poetry and eat lots of food. Most to the point of regret. On both. Though my talents seem to be scrambled, the internet has found a way to aggregate my nonsense, so for the most part, I'm failry searchable.

To be honest, I dont know how to write about myself in a positive manner (or any manner), So I'll probably rewrite this at some point in the future when I have something better drafted. Consider this a fancy, long-whinded Lorem Ipsum.
