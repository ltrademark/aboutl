follow toast

We make our own bread and then create wonderful toasts. From the simple cinnamon sugar to big smoked whitefish tartines. Come enjoy some Toast!

This is our 3rd restaurant as we also own Handsome Biscuit (diagonally across the street) and Field Guide (downtown Norfolk next to the Granby Theater). Currently we are still developing our outdoor seating here at Toast as well as building our menu to be ready for breakfast lunch hours. Stay posted as we hope to add these by the summer.
